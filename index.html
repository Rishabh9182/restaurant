Raj Kachori

Raj Kachori is a popular and indulgent Indian street food snack known for its vibrant flavors and textures. Hailing from the North Indian cuisine, particularly Rajasthan, it is a large, deep-fried, puffed puri made from semolina or flour dough. The puri is typically filled with a mixture of various ingredients, including boiled potatoes, sprouted moong beans, chickpeas, yogurt, chutneys (such as tamarind and mint), and spices.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a beloved South Indian dish that has gained popularity worldwide. It is a type of fermented crepe or pancake made from a batter of rice and urad dal (black gram) and is known for its crispiness. The dosa is typically thin and large, and it is served with a filling known as "masala," which is a mixture of spiced and seasoned mashed potatoes.
